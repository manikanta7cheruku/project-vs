About Vastu

Vastu Shastra, the ancient science of architecture, is based on balancing five core elements to harmonize living spaces.

Earth (Prithvi)

Represents stability and foundation of structures.

Water (Jal)

Symbolizes flow, adaptability, and purification.

Fire (Agni)

Governs energy, transformation, and clarity.

Air (Vayu)

Controls movement, circulation, and freshness.

Space (Akasha)

Provides freedom, expansion, and openness.
